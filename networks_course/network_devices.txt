Network:
A group or system of interconnected nodes that share resources

Client (AKA endpoints, end hosts):
A device that accesses a service made available by a server

Server:
Any client can be a server, however, for a device to act as a server,
it must provide functions or services for other clients.

Switch
Provides connectivity to hosts within the same LAN (Local area network)
Has many network ports for end hosts to connect to (usually 24+)
Do not provides connectivity between LANs/over the internet

Router
Has fewer network interfaces than switches
Provides connectivity between LANs
